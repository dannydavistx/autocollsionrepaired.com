Paintless Dent Repair

The original factory paint on most standard vehicles is flexible, in other words dents can often be repaired without painting depending on the depth and location of the damage. Custom Auto Body uses best practices when using these methods to bring your vehicle to pre-accident condition.

Cracked or Broken Glass

Whether your windshield needs a complete replacement or is cracked or shattered and needs to be skillfully repaired, bring your vehicle to the experts at Custom Auto Body. We can help ensure your vehicle’s safety and offer you peace of mind.

Car Bumper and Car Fender

At Custom Auto Body, our team meticulously performs the right repair for each bumper, and reconditions your bumper to erase any signs of dents, scratches, scrapes, or indentations. Many plastic bumper parts can be repaired, especially bumper covers, which are commonly damaged on a vehicle. By saving your bumper, the technician will also be able to blend the appended paint more efficiently and accurately.

New Paint or Spot Paint

Paint chips can result from rocks or gravel thrown up by a car in front of you, inclement weather, or from a collision. They’re often hard to cover up with simple touch-up paint, and can detract from the appearance of your vehicle. Luckily, the team at Custom Auto Body boasts the industry’s finest painters and staff to get your vehicle’s paint finish looking like new again.
