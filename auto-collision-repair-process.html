Our 7-Step Repair Process

1

Vehicle Drop Off

If you’ve been in an accident, or your vehicle has damage that you would like repaired by the very best in the business, simply call our office or drop off your vehicle at our Fort Worth location. Our team works side-by-side with leading insurance providers to complete a free computerized estimate and accurately assess any damage before we begin making repairs.

2

Body Repair

After your vehicle is evaluated, it moves into the body shop, where we remove all parts requiring separate repair or replacement, and perform body and structural repair work. Meanwhile, all repair work undergoes a rigorous quality control inspection.

3

Paint/Polish

Once body work is complete, your vehicle begins the painting and polish process. First, our collision repair artists utilize computer technology to find the exact color match based on manufacturer codes before applying fresh paint. Your vehicle is then polished and prepared for reassembly before entering another quality control checkpoint.

4

Reassembly

Your vehicle returns to the body shop where it is reassembled with lamps, air conditioning, grills, doors, and other parts involved in your repair. Once assembled, it enters a final quality control check to ensure all authorized repair work is completed and that the job reflects our standard of excellence.

5

Clean Vehicle

Our masterful work isn’t done until we treat your vehicle to a complete wash and vacuum. This final step ensures your vehicle will look emaculate upon pick up and allows us to check cosmetic repairs one last time.

6

Quality Check

The safety and complete satisfaction of our customers is our number one priority. That’s why we’ve developed and implemented one of the most rigorous quality control methods in the industry. When you leave your vehicle with us, we’ll hand back the keys hassle-free once your vehicle is returned to its pre-accident condition. That's how we roll...Custom Auto Body Repair  - We Pay Your Insurance Deductible

7

Vehicle Ready

As soon as your repair is complete, one of our customer success representatives will contact you and invite you in for the final inspection and paperwork. Your customer success representative will review the vehicle and repairs side-by-side before completing the final documentation and handing back your keys.

Finally, you’ll have peace of mind driving away with a rock solid lifetime warranty, that's a written promise backing all of our repair work free of charge in the rare event you aren't completely satisfied with our work.
