Collision Repair

Custom Auto Body is committed to making the collision repair process simple and stress free. Our Collision Repair Services are the most comprehensive auto body repair services in the industry. Every customer is offered the following benefits:

1

Personal Assistant

Your single point of contact throughout the entire collision repair process. Think of it as your go to person that handles all the scheduling, paperwork, and communications with everyone.

2

Rental Car Assistance

We can help you reserve a reliable rental vehicle that can be billed back to most major insurance companies. Also, we can have your vehicle brought to you and schedule dropping it off as well. 

3

24 Hour Towing

We can schedule a reputable towing service for you 24 hours a day 7 days a week. In addition, we have a courtesy driver who can give you a ride home or to work.

4

Auto Detailing

Every collision repair comes with our Platinum detailing service. In addition to your vehicle looking shiny and new enables you to check for any cosmetic repairs one last time.
